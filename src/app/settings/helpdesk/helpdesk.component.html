Setting up your helpdesk

All basic settings of your helpdesk can be customized here such as - language, time zone and colour schemes.

If you are on the Garden plan or above, you will have the option to set up multilingual support under Languages.

To configure the portal for your product, use the Edit Portal option.

Helpdesk restrictions

You can restrict the access to your helpdesk to the contacts that you add and to certain domains. Others will not be able to sign up, log in or submit tickets. You can read more about it here.
